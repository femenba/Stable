### Day 18 — Thursday
**Feed `C`:** *"The ADHD emotional experience that nobody talks about."*
Topic: Rejection Sensitive Dysphoria (RSD)
Slide 1: Cover — "The emotion nobody told you was an ADHD symptom."
Slide 2: What RSD is — intense emotional response to perceived rejection/criticism
Slide 3: How it shows up — overreacting, avoiding feedback, fear of failure, people-pleasing
Slide 4: Why it happens — amygdala dysregulation + dopamine sensitivity
Slide 5: What helps — naming it, talking about it, building emotional safety, stable. mood tracking
Slide 6: "You're not too sensitive. You're not dramatic. Your nervous system is wired differently."
**Story:** "Do you experience RSD? How does it show up for you?" question box
**Pillar:** Education / Emotional Depth
