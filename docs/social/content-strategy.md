# @stableadhd — Content Strategy

## Content Pillars

Nine pillars govern every piece of content. Each post must serve one primary pillar.

### 1. ADHD Education
Factual, research-backed content that reframes ADHD — not as deficit, but as difference.
Tone: authoritative but accessible. Never condescending.
Format: Carousels, quote posts, infographic-style cards.

### 2. Emotional Validation
The content that makes people stop scrolling and whisper "this is me."
Tone: Deeply personal, honest, no toxic positivity.
Format: Single image with quote, story polls, raw text posts.

### 3. Focus Systems
Practical frameworks that actually work for ADHD brains.
Tone: Practical, calm, no-hustle energy.
Format: Carousels (step-by-step), reels (demonstrations).

### 4. Routines
Not "morning routines of successful CEOs." Real, flexible, forgiving routines.
Tone: Realistic, non-judgmental, iterative.
Format: Carousels, before/after story sets.

### 5. Task Paralysis
Naming the thing that nobody talks about.
Tone: Validating first, then gently practical.
Format: Quote posts, short reels, carousel breakdowns.

### 6. Self-Compassion
Permission to rest. Permission to not optimize. Permission to be human.
Tone: Soft, generous, healing.
Format: Quote posts, slow reels, story cards.

### 7. Calm Productivity
The anti-hustle approach to getting things done.
Tone: Philosophical, grounded, quiet confidence.
Format: Long-form carousels, reels.

### 8. App Features
Product content that doesn't feel like advertising.
Tone: Practical demonstration, outcome-focused.
Format: Screen recording reels, feature carousels, story highlights.

### 9. Build in Public
The human behind stable. — decisions, failures, learnings.
Tone: Transparent, honest, no performance.
Format: Single posts, long captions, story Q&As.

## Launch Philosophy

We don't launch with noise. We launch with intention.

The first 9 posts on an Instagram profile are a portfolio — a single designed object. Every post must serve the whole.

**Goal of launch week:** not followers, but resonance. We want the right 1,000 people to find this account and feel like it was made for them. The algorithm rewards saves and shares more than likes — so every piece of content must earn a save.

---

## Engagement Protocol (Manual — First 90 Days)

- Reply to every comment within 2 hours of posting
- DM every new follower who engages with a story poll
- Comment on 5–10 posts daily in adjacent hashtags (#ADHDlife, #neurodivergent)
- Engage with ADHD creators weekly (builds referral traffic)
- Never use generic replies ("Great post!") — always specific, human, warm

### Prompt 03 — Reel Script
```
You are the creative director of @stableadhd.

Write a 20-30 second Instagram Reel script. Text-on-screen format OR voiceover format (specify which).

Hook requirement: First 2 seconds must stop the scroll. Use a specific, relatable ADHD moment — not a question.

Structure:
- Hook (2s): Visual or statement that lands immediately
- Problem (6s): Name the ADHD experience precisely
- Insight (8s): The reframe or education piece
- Resolution (8s): How stable. helps (never hard-sell)
- CTA (3s): One action

Voice: Calm, measured, intelligent. No filler words. No "hey guys."
Audio direction: [ambient / lo-fi / silence / trending audio category]

Topic: [TOPIC]
```
