## Usage Rules

- **15–20 tags per post** — Instagram treats 30 as a spam signal
- **Placement:** Always in the caption, after a line break — never in first comment
- **Never use:** #hustle #grind #motivationmonday (wrong brand energy)
- **Never use:** Tags with 50M+ posts (you'll be invisible)
- **Never use:** Irrelevant tags for impressions — it damages reach
